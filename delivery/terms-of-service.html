1. Independent Contractor Relationship

1.1 Nature of Relationship

You acknowledge and agree that:

  • You are an independent contractor, NOT an employee of FlipStore
  • This Agreement does not create an employer-employee relationship
  • You are NOT entitled to employee benefits (PF, ESI, gratuity, paid leave)
  • You control when, where, and how long you work (subject to platform availability)
  • You provide your own vehicle, fuel, and equipment
  • FlipStore does not withhold income tax; you are responsible for your own taxes

1.2 Freedom to Accept/Reject

  • You have complete freedom to accept or reject delivery requests
  • No minimum delivery requirements (though incentives may have thresholds)
  • You can work with other delivery platforms simultaneously
  • You determine your own working hours and availability

2. Eligibility and Registration

2.1 Eligibility Criteria

To register as a delivery partner, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Hold a valid driving license (for motorized vehicles)
  • Own or have legal access to a delivery vehicle (bike, scooter, bicycle, car)
  • Have valid vehicle insurance (third-party liability minimum)
  • Have valid vehicle registration and pollution certificate
  • Possess a valid government-issued ID (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
  • Have a smartphone with GPS and internet connectivity
  • Provide a valid bank account for payment settlements

2.2 Registration Process

During registration, you must provide:

  • Personal information (name, DOB, contact details)
  • Identity proof and address proof
  • Driving license and vehicle registration documents
  • Vehicle insurance documents
  • Bank account details (for payouts)
  • Profile photo

2.3 Background Verification

  • FlipStore reserves the right to conduct background verification
  • We may verify documents with issuing authorities
  • We may check for criminal records or traffic violations
  • Approval typically takes 2-5 business days
  • We reserve the right to reject applications without stating reasons

2.4 Account Approval

  • Upon approval, you will receive login credentials
  • You must keep your account information up-to-date
  • Notify us immediately of any changes to documents (expired license, insurance)

3. Delivery Services

3.1 Service Description

As a delivery partner, you agree to:

  • Pick up orders from vendor locations
  • Deliver orders to customer addresses
  • Ensure safe and timely delivery of products
  • Collect cash payments (COD) when applicable
  • Provide proof of delivery (photos, OTP, signature)
  • Handle products with care to prevent damage

3.2 Accepting Delivery Requests

  • You will receive delivery requests based on your location and availability
  • Each request shows: pickup location, delivery location, distance, estimated earnings
  • You have [X] seconds to accept or reject a request
  • Acceptance rate affects your priority for future requests (though not mandatory)

3.3 Order Pickup

When picking up orders, you must:

  • Arrive at the vendor location within the estimated time
  • Verify the order details and items
  • Mark the order as "Picked Up" in the app
  • Check for special handling instructions (fragile, keep upright, etc.)
  • Ensure proper packaging to prevent damage during transit

3.4 Delivery Process

When delivering orders, you must:

  • Navigate to the customer's delivery address
  • Contact the customer if you cannot locate the address
  • Hand over the order to the customer or authorized person
  • Collect payment (for COD orders)
  • Obtain proof of delivery (photo, OTP, or signature)
  • Mark the order as "Delivered" in the app

3.5 Delivery Timelines

  • Deliver orders within the estimated delivery time shown in the app
  • Notify customer and support if delays occur
  • Late deliveries may affect your performance metrics

4. Earnings and Payments

4.1 Delivery Fee Structure

You will earn delivery fees based on:

  • Base Fee: Fixed amount per delivery (varies by region)
  • Distance: Additional fee based on km traveled
  • Time: Additional fee for time taken (if applicable)
  • Surge Pricing: Higher fees during peak hours or high demand
  • Weather Bonus: Extra pay during adverse weather (rain, extreme heat)

4.2 Incentives and Bonuses

  • Daily Targets: Complete X deliveries to earn bonus INR Y
  • Weekly Streaks: Consistent performance bonuses
  • High Ratings: Bonuses for maintaining 4.5+ star rating
  • Referral Bonuses: Earn when you refer new delivery partners

4.3 Payment Schedule

  • Earnings are settled on a [daily/weekly] basis
  • Payments transferred to your registered bank account via NEFT/RTGS/UPI
  • Payment cycles: Every [day of week] for previous week's deliveries
  • Minimum payout threshold: INR [amount]
  • If earnings are below threshold, they roll over to next cycle

4.4 COD (Cash on Delivery) Settlement

  • You must collect exact cash from customers for COD orders
  • Do NOT provide change from your own pocket (customer must have exact amount or use digital payment)
  • COD collected is deducted from your next payout
  • You must remit COD within [X] days (automatically deducted)
  • Discrepancies in COD amounts will be investigated

4.5 Deductions

The following may be deducted from your earnings:

  • COD amounts collected from customers
  • Penalties for policy violations (see Section 8)
  • Compensation for damaged or lost orders (if you are at fault)
  • Outstanding dues or chargebacks

4.6 Tax Responsibilities

  • You are responsible for all tax obligations (Income Tax, GST if applicable)
  • FlipStore may deduct TDS as per Indian tax laws
  • Annual earnings statements will be provided for your tax filing
  • Consult a tax professional for guidance on gig economy taxation

6. Performance Standards and Expectations

6.1 Performance Metrics

We track the following performance indicators:

  • Acceptance Rate: % of delivery requests accepted
  • Completion Rate: % of accepted deliveries completed
  • On-Time Delivery Rate: % of deliveries completed within estimated time
  • Customer Rating: Average rating (1-5 stars)
  • Cancellation Rate: % of accepted deliveries cancelled
  • Order Handling: % of orders delivered without damage or issues

6.2 Expected Standards

To maintain active delivery partner status, aim for:

  • Acceptance rate: No mandatory minimum (freedom to reject)
  • Completion rate: ≥ 90% (once accepted)
  • On-time delivery rate: ≥ 85%
  • Customer rating: ≥ 4.0/5.0
  • Cancellation rate: ≤ 5%

6.3 Consequences of Poor Performance

  • Lower priority in delivery request allocation
  • Ineligibility for certain incentives and bonuses
  • Warning notifications and improvement plans
  • Temporary suspension for repeated violations
  • Account termination for consistently poor performance

7. Conduct and Professional Behavior

7.1 Professional Conduct

As a delivery partner, you must:

  • Be polite, respectful, and courteous to customers and vendors
  • Dress appropriately and maintain personal hygiene
  • Follow traffic rules and drive/ride safely
  • Handle products with care to prevent damage
  • Maintain confidentiality of customer information
  • Not consume alcohol or drugs while on duty

7.2 Customer Interaction

  • Communicate professionally if you need to contact customers
  • Do NOT harass, abuse, or behave inappropriately with customers
  • Do NOT request tips or extra payment
  • Do NOT use customer contact information for personal purposes

7.3 Safety and Security

  • Follow all road safety rules and wear helmets (for two-wheelers)
  • Do NOT compromise safety to meet delivery deadlines
  • Report any accidents or incidents immediately
  • Do NOT tamper with, steal, or misuse customer orders
